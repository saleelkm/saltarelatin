About Us

About Us

SaltareLatin is a dance academy located in Bangalore, India that specializes in Latin dance styles such as Salsa, Bachata, and Merengue. The academy offers a variety of classes for all skill levels taught by experienced and passionate instructors. SaltareLatin's goal is to promote the joy of Latin dance and culture through high-quality instruction and a fun and inclusive atmosphere.
